PROCEDURES

1. ACCOMPLISH YOUR APPLICATION FORM

Visit an NSO Office or access the PSA website to obtain a copy of the application form. The accomplished form may be submitted to the screeners' desk or online. For online applications, provide your TIN and accurate delivery details.

2. PAY FOR YOUR APPLICATION Payments may be made to the cashier or collecting officer for walk-in applications, or using VISA, Mastercard, Bancnet ATM, or Globe GCash, or via Metrobank or Bayad Center for online applications. 3. WAIT FOR YOUR MARRIAGE CERTIFICATE Walk-in applicants will receive their marriage certificate within 1 hour. Depending on the delivery conditions, online applicants will need to wait a few days to receive their document.

REQUIREMENTS

• Any valid ID • Accomplished Application Form
