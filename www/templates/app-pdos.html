General

Filipino emigrants or those leaving the country to settle permanently abroad are required to register with the Commission on Filipinos Overseas. Part of its registration requirements is attendance in the Pre-Departure Orientation Seminar(PDOS) to prepare them for settlement overseas.

Procedures

1. Go to the website

Click the "I already have..." button if you have already secured your immigrant visa.

2. Answer preliminary questions

Answer all the preliminary questions to check if required to attend PDOS or Peer Counseling Program. Provide all email address and click "submit".

3. Confirm via email

Open email and look for confirmation message. Click on "Confirm Email" and provide information as specified in the "Personal Information" page.

4. Get Advise to take PDOS/Peer

You will be advised if required to take PDOS/Peer or if you are exempted.

A. To Attend PDOS/Peer

If required to attend PDOS, you will be directed to the "SET YOUR SCHEDULE" page. Click the calendar to select preferred date and time to attend the PDOS/PEER session. Note: Limited slots per session, per day.

B. Exempted (Proceed to Step 5)

5. Complete online registration

Click "Proceed to registration" button to complete the on-line registration form.

6. Upload information

Upload your recent clear photo and supply correct and complete information in all the fields required in the registration form.

7. Print confirmation form

Download and print the barcoded confirmation form. Bring this document together with all the other requirements to CFO on date of appointment with all the other requirements to CFO on date of appointment. NO NEED TO PRINT your registration form. Please come to CFO at least 2 hours before your scheduled PDOS/PEER session for the verification process.
