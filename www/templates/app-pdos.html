General Procedures Requirements

General

Filipino emigrants or those leaving the country to settle permanently abroad are required to register with the Commission on Filipinos Overseas. Part of its registration requirements is attendance in the Pre-Departure Orientation Seminar(PDOS) to prepare them for settlement overseas.

Procedures

1. Register

Sign up and log-in with your details to access the learning modules in the websites. Existing members of Jobstreet.com may also use their account to access the PEOS online.

2. Take the learning modules

Complete all of the eight (8) self-learning modules. Each module takes approximately seven (7) minutes to complete.

3. Review your learnings

Take the review at the end of each module. Answer all of the questions and get at least 3 correct answers out of the 5 questions to proceed.

4. Print your PEOS certificates

Save and print a copy of your PEOS certificate and include this in your pre-employment processing with POEA.
