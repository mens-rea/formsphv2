Procedures Requirements

PROCEDURES

1. Bring the necessary requirements and head to a DTI office

Bring the requirements and fill out the application form if you don’t have one yet, DTI will provide the form for you.

2. Pay the fees

Pay the necessary fees applicable for your business.
Barangay P100
City/ Municipality P500
Regional P1000
National P2000

3. Wait to claim your DTI Business registration

Wait for few minutes for your DTI business permit or DTI business certificate. You will also receive an email from the DTI confirming your business registration.

REQUIREMENTS

Applicant must be at least 18 years of age. 1 valid ID (SSS ID, GSIS, Voter’s ID, PRC license, Driver’s License, Passport or any issued government issued ID) Application Form (you can download here) Registration Fee per territorial scheme
