General

UMID a.k.a. Unified Multi-purpose ID is the single ID card for all SSS, GSIS, Pagibig and Philhealth members so it is important to have one. How to get and apply for UMID card? It is simple and very easy. You cannot get or apply UMID online in the moment as you need to do it personally.

UMID system is in compliance with Executive Order No. 420 dated April 13, 2005 which required all government agencies and government owned and controlled corporations (GOCCs) to streamline and harmonize their identification systems, and Executive Order No. 700 dated January 16, 2008 which identified the social security identification system as the core of the UMID system and directed the SSS to implement the streamlining and harmonization of the ID systems of all government agencies and GOCCs. GSIS is the first to adopt the Unified Multi-purpose ID card.

Address



Solicarel Bldg. R. Magsaysay Blvd. Sta. Mesa Manila, San Juan, Metro Manila

Procedures

1. Visit any of the folllowing government agencies:

Social Security System (SSS) – See all SSS Offices within Metro Manila
PhilHealth
Government Service Insurance System (GSIS)
Pag-Ibig – See all Pag-Ibig Offices within Metro Manila



2. Fill out the form

Forms are available at enrollment centers . It can also be downloaded online via GSIS (government employee) or SSS website (private employee or self-employed or OFW).



3. Verify UMID form

Submit your form along with your valid IDs.



4. ID capture, fingerprint and signature

Once you’ve got verified, you’ll be asked to transfer to another window for an ID picture, to scan your fingerprints and to sign on the signature pad.



5. Wait for UMID card to arrive by mail

After your ID capture, you will have to wait for 30 days to receive your UMID ID by mail. If you fail to receive the ID after 30 days, inquire to your nearest SSS Branch Office.



Requirements

UMID application form Any one (1) primary document
  • Driver's License
  • Passport
  • Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) card
  • Seaman's Book
