General

The GSIS eCard is, by far, the most innovative government-issued identification card today. This is because the eCard Plus is not just a GSIS membership ID Card, it is also a GSIS transactional card, disbursement card, ATM Card, VISA debit card, hospitalization discount card, medicine discount card, and tuition discount card, among others.

Procedures

1. GO TO A GSIS OFFICE

Obtain and accomplish the eCard enrollment form, and submit it to the GSIS personnel assigned in the eCard enrollment area.




2. CLAIM YOUR ECARD

You can either provide the GSIS office your delivery information and recieve your eCard through mail, or claim the eCard yourself from the GSIS office you applied in.



3. VISIT A G-W@PS KIOSK

Upon receiving your eCard

Go to any GSIS Wireless Activated Processing System (G-W@PS) kiosk located at the nearest GSIS or major government office.



4. ACTIVATE YOUR ECARD

To Activate your eCard

A. Place your eCard in the G-W@PS Kiosk card reader.

B. Touch the screen to select any of your pre-registered fingers.

C. Very lightly, put the finger you selected on the fingerprint scanner to activate your eCard

Additional Information:

A. If the finger you selected fails to get scanned, simply select another pre-registered finger.

B. You only need to activate your card once after you receive it.

You can use your eCard after five (5) working days after activating it. You can check your membership account or apply for loans using your eCard Plus in any G-W@PS kiosk nearest you.
