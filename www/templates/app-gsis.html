Procedures

1. GO TO THE NEAREST GSIS OFFICE

A. Bring two valid IDs (examples: company ID, LTO license, PRC license, passport, voter's registration ID) to the nearest GSIS office nearest you.

B. Accomplish an eCard enrollment form and submit it to the GSIS personnel assigned in the eCard enrollment area.

C. In a few weeks, you can either get your eCard from the GSIS office where you applied or through snail mail




2. CLAIM YOUR ECARD

Upon receiving your eCard

A. Go to any GSIS Wireless Activated Processing System (G-W@PS) kiosk located at the nearest GSIS or major government office.

B. Place your eCard in the G-W@PS Kiosk card reader.

C. Touch the screen to select any of your pre-registered fingers.

D. Very lightly, put the finger you selected on the fingerprint scanner to activate your eCard

3. OTHER INFORMATION

Some friendly reminders

A. If the finger you selected fails to get scanned, simply select another pre-registered finger.

B. You only need to activate your card once after you receive it.

You can use your eCard after five (5) working days after activating it. You can check your membership account or apply for loans using your eCard Plus in any G-W@PS kiosk nearest you.
