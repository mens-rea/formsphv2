PROCEDURES

1. Applicant or care-giver must fill out the forms

Find the barangay secretary and inform them that you need a barangay clearance. Be prepared to answer a few short questions about your residency and purpose for getting a barangay clearance.

2. Supply your personal data

Fill out and complete an application form with accurate details. Some barangays will only ask for your signature while others barangay-require a thumb print and will even take your picture.

3. Pay for your application

After completing the application form and supplying your personal details, submit all the barangay-requirements and pay the barangay-processing fees. It is advised that you bring between P50-P100. Once paid, just wait for 20 to 30 minutes and you will be given your barangay clearance.

REQUIREMENTS

Filled up application form Recent Cedula (community tax certificate) Application fees (price may vary in every barangay) (Optional) bring at least one (1) valid ID
