General

Undergo a Pre-Employment Orientation Seminar (PEOS) online as a pre-qualification requirement by the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA).

New applicants should take the PEOS Online prior to submission of his application to a licensed recruitment agency. Likewise, the recruitment agency should submit for POEA processing only documents of workers who have completed the online PEOS.

You can take the POEA Pre-employment Orientation Seminar Online for free. If you have completed the modules provided in the online seminar, POEA will issue you a PEOS Certificate which you can use as an additional certificate when applying a job and you can include it in your pre-employment processing with POEA. PEOS Online have eight modules. Each module including the assessment test takes an average of seven minutes to complete.

Procedures

1. Register

Sign up and log-in with your details to access the learning modules in the websites. Existing members of Jobstreet.com may also use their account to access the PEOS online.

2. Take the learning modules

Complete all of the eight (8) self-learning modules. Each module takes approximately seven (7) minutes to complete.

3. Review your learnings

Take the review at the end of each module. Answer all of the questions and get at least 3 correct answers out of the 5 questions to proceed.

4. Print your PEOS certificates

Save and print a copy of your PEOS certificate and include this in your pre-employment processing with POEA.
