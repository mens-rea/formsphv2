REQUIREMENTS

Two (2) valid IDs Printed Application Form

PROCEDURES

1. COMPLETE THE ONLINE APPLICATION FORM Visit the NBI website and fill out the online application form. Ensure that all information is valid and accurate. After completing the form, a confirmation email will be sent to the email address that you provided. 2. SET AN APPOINTMENT WITH THE NBI The second part of the online application is scheduling an appointment. Look for the NBI branch where you wish to have your clearance processed, and input your preferred date and time. Make sure to indicate the purpose of your NBI clearance. The fee will depend on the purpose of your application. 3. PREPARE THE REQUIREMENTS Print out your application form and take note of the Reference Code that will be generated from the website after you set your schedule. Prepare two (2) valid IDs as well. 4. SHOW UP FOR PROCESSING Go to your intended NBI branch and present your valid IDs and reference code, then pay for your clearance. Have your photo and biometrics taken, and wait in the printing area to get your NBI clearance.
