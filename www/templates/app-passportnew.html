REQUIREMENTS

• NSO Birth Certificate (original copy) • At least one (1) valid ID, or at least three (3) documents that proves your identity • Additional two (2) photocopies of each document • Accomplished Application Form

PROCEDURES

1. MAKE AN APPOINTMENT WITH THE DFA To set an appointment with the DFA, you can either go to the nearest DFA office or visit the DFA Appointment Website. Answer the application form, and a reference number will be given to you after submission. Whether done online or in person, have your accomplished application form printed. 2. PREPARE ALL REQUIRED DOCUMENTS Ensure that all the general requirements are ready before your appointment with the DFA. In case of no birth record, other documents may be required as a substitute for the birth certificate. 3. VISIT THE DFA OFFICE Go to the DFA Consular Office on your scheduled date and time. If applicable, visit the most convenient DFA satellites and Philippine Embassy/Consulate as per your appointment. Do not forget to present your application form and wait for your turn. 4. PAY FOR YOUR APPLICATION Proceed to the cashier or the payment section to settle your application and processing fees. 5. HAVE YOUR PHOTO AND OTHER INFORMATION TAKEN Proceed to the encoding section and have your photo and other information taken by the staff. 6. WAIT FOR YOUR PASSPORT The processing of your passport can take up to thirty (30) days. If available, you can proceed to the delivery counter and provide your delivery information, so you can have the passport delivered to you. You may also opt to claim your passport in person.
