General

Death Certificate is an official document setting forth particulars relating to a dead person, including the name of the individual, the date of birth and the date of death.

When requesting for death certificate, the interested party shall provide the following information to facilitate verification and issuance of certification.

Address



Solicarel Bldg. R. Magsaysay Blvd. Sta. Mesa Manila, San Juan, Metro Manila

Procedures

1. Accomplish application form

Visit a Serbilis Outlet or access the PSA website to obtain a copy of the application form. The accomplished form may be submitted to the screener's desk or online. For online applications, provide your TIN and accurate delivery details.

Provide the following information:

Number of copies being death-requested
Name of the deceased
Gender
Place of death
Date of death
Date of registration, if the death certificate was registered late
Purpose of your death-request


You can go to your nearest NSO branch to submit your form or



2. Pay Application Fees

Payments may be made to the cashier or collecting officer for walk-in applications, or using VISA, Mastercard, Bancnet ATM, or Globe GCash, or via Metrobank or Bayad Center for online applications.



3. Claim the death certificate

Walk in applicants will recieve the death certificate within 1 hour. Depending on the delivery conditions, online applicants will need to wait a few days to recieve their document.

Requirements

One (1) valid ID Accomplished application form Processing Fee - Php330/copy
