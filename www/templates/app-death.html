General Procedures Requirements

General

Death Certificate is an official document setting forth particulars relating to a dead person, including the name of the individual, the date of birth and the date of death.

When requesting for death certificate, the interested party shall provide the following information to facilitate verification and issuance of certification.

Address



Solicarel Bldg. R. Magsaysay Blvd. Sta. Mesa Manila, San Juan, Metro Manila

Procedures

1. Fill up application form


Provide the following information:

Number of copies being death-requested
Name of the deceased
Gender
Place of death
Date of death
Date of registration, if the death certificate was registered late
Purpose of your death-request

Requirements

One (1) valid ID 330php per copy
