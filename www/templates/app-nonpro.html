Procedures Requirements

PROCEDURES

1. Full up a driver's license application form:

Go to the customer service counter to get your checklist of drivers-requirements and secure a driver’s license application form. [This form is available for download here. You can accomplish this form prior to transacting your business at the LTO.] Get a queue number.

2. Submit all drivers-required documents

When your number is called, go to the evaluator counter and submit all the drivers-required documents and have it checked for completeness and authenticity.

3. Get your photo and signature taken.

When your number is called, go to the evaluator counter and submit all the drivers-required documents and have it checked for completeness and authenticity.

3. Pay the application fee

Go to the cashier to pay the application fee.

4. Take the lecture and written exam

Go to the Examination Room for the lecture and written exam.

5. Take the practical exam

After passing the written exam, wait for your name to be called for the practical exam.

6. Pay other necessary fees

After passing the practical exam, go to the cashier when your name is called for payment of necessary fees and obtain an official receipt (OR).

7. Claim your license

Go to the releasing counter, wait for your name to be called and present the OR and claim the card type license.

REQUIREMENTS

Duly accomplished application form for driver’s license (ADL) [Download this form here]. Valid student permit (at least one month old) Medical certificate with official receipt from any duly-licensed and practicing physician (Note: The LTO will not accept medical certificates which are over 15 days old.) Negative drug test result (from a DOH accredited drug testing center & Government Hospital) If employed: Taxpayer’s Identification Number (TIN) Must have passed the written and practical examinations*
